The Smart Tie is a stackable engineered profile made from the patented I-Rock process. The Smart Tie comes in lengths up to 12’ long and can be cut to size of your custom application with conventional wood working tools. The Smart Tie is designed to stack between lightweight 6” steel I-Beams that are bolted to the surface floor.

The Smart Tie can be used in many different applications from small jobs like protective knee walls to sound barriers for expressways. The wall system can be set up assembled with no need for a skilled or trade labor like block walls. Also the wall can be taken down, relocated and reconstructed as many times as you like, there is no mortar or glue. The “Smart” in Smart Tie is; reduced labor, installation time, cost, and use of potentially dangerous treated lumber.
