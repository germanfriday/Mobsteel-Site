The I-Rock paver is an end cut piece from our 7x9 railroad tie profile. By end cutting the timber we expose the trapped recycled material leaving a terrazzo, granite lock. I-Rock pavers are an alternate to conventional brick pavers at less cost and easier handling. Min in our 4"4" to create a unique pattern.
